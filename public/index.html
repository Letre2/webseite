    About Tobias Ertel

    In my work as a designer, I strive to shape the world we want to live in - one that is also sustainable, one that works with us and for us. I like to rethink processes that are unsustainable or don‘t make sense for us humans. There is always a better way, and I have made it my mission to look for it. In the future, I would like to work for people and with people, while not losing sight of design creation. It is all about people, rather than technology. I am curious about interactive objects - both digital and physical -
