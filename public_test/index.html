    About Tobias Ertel

    I am an interdisciplinary Interaction Design student and photographer from south Germany. In the future, I would like to work with people for the common good, while not losing sight of design. Working with human-machine interfaces is more than designing websites and apps for me. I am curious about interactive objects - both digital and physical.
